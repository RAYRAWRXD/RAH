JOURNAL: FORAGING + RESISTANCE

April 28, 2023

Today I went foraging in the abandoned lot behind the old factory. Found some amazing dandelions, plantain, and even some wild strawberries pushing through the cracks in the pavement. Nature finds a way.

Later, worked on my zine about urban rewilding. Remember: every patch of weeds is a rebellion against concrete domination. Documenting these small victories feels like archiving the revolution.

DIY NATURE INK RECIPE

March 15, 2023

Made some radical plant-based inks today:

  • Black: Oak galls + iron sulfate
  • Yellow: Goldenrod flowers
  • Green: Nettle + alum mordant

The colors are fugitive but that's part of the beauty - temporary like seasons. Used them to screenprint some anti-capitalist posters. The police can't stop what biodegrades!
